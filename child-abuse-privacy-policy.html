Child Safety Standards Policy

Last updated on January 22, 2025

At Koottukaari, we are committed to ensuring a safe and secure environment for all our users. As a dating app designed exclusively for adults, we adhere strictly to Google Play’s child safety standards to prevent child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) on our platform.

Our Commitment

We take the following measures to comply with child safety standards:

  • Published guidelines against child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).
  • An in-app mechanism for users to report inappropriate or abusive behavior.
  • Immediate action to address and remove child sexual abuse material (CSAM).
  • Strict compliance with child safety laws and regulations.
  • A dedicated child safety point of contact for reporting concerns.

Zero Tolerance for CSAE

Koottukaari maintains a zero-tolerance policy for CSAE, including but not limited to:

  • Grooming a child for sexual exploitation.
  • Sextortion or trafficking of minors.
  • Any behavior that endangers or exploits children.

Reporting and Feedback

We encourage users to report any suspicious or inappropriate behavior through our in-app reporting tool. Every report is thoroughly investigated, and necessary actions are taken, including contacting law enforcement when required.

Compliance with Laws

We strictly adhere to all applicable child safety laws and regulations. Our team undergoes regular training to stay updated on best practices for combating online child exploitation.

Child Safety Contact

If you suspect any child-related abuse or have concerns, please contact us immediately at childsafety@koottukaari.com. Your reports are confidential and will be handled with utmost seriousness.

Policy Updates

We may update this policy to remain compliant with evolving laws and standards. Updates will be communicated via email or posted on this page. Please review this policy periodically for the latest information.
